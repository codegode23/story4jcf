ABOUT US

Jireh Charity Foundation is an NGO consisting of well intended individuals,

who's main aim is to provide aids to less privileged individuals mainly orphaned children and widows by providing them with basic needs such as food, clothing, educational materials, vocational skills amongst others. This is to help alleviate their burdens, make their lives better and help equip them with necessary life skills. We are a registered NGO with the Registrar General

We aid in bettering the lives of the less privileged and orphaned. We do this by providing basic needs, materials to help enrich their and smiles on their faces through love and unity. Our Organization primarily focuses on children between the ages of zero to eighteen years.

JCF is committed to bringing people together to help mobilize funds and resources to be donated to various children charity homes. These include educational items such as books, pens, pencils, school bags, shoes and school clothes. We also provide hygiene products such as toiletries, sanitary pads amongst others. We regularly organize health screening for children at the Homes.
